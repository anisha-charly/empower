Community Resources

Sakshi

Sakshi is a non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Delhi that works towards creating awareness and providing support for survivors of sexual harassment and assault. They offer counseling services, legal aid, and conduct workshops on consent education.

SRAINN (Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network) India

RAINN India is a helpline that provides confidential support and resources to survivors of sexual violence. They offer counseling, legal assistance, and referrals to local support services..

Lawyers Collective

Lawyers Collective is a legal advocacy organization that provides legal aid and support to survivors of gender-based violence, including sexual harassment and assault. They offer legal representation, advocacy, and training on legal rights and procedures

Maitri India:

Maitri India is an NGO based in Mumbai that works on issues related to gender-based violence and women's rights. They provide counseling, legal aid, and support services for survivors of domestic violence and sexual assault.

Nirbhaya Jyoti Trust

Nirbhaya Jyoti Trust is an organization founded by the parents of Jyoti Singh, the victim of the 2012 Delhi gang-rape case. They work towards supporting survivors of sexual violence through counseling, rehabilitation, and advocacy for legal reforms..

Enfold India:

Enfold India is an NGO based in Bangalore that focuses on child protection and prevention of child sexual abuse. They conduct workshops and training programs for parents, educators, and professionals on recognizing signs of abuse and teaching consent education to children.

Women's Helpline (181):

The Women's Helpline is a national helpline number in India that provides support and assistance to women facing various forms of violence, including sexual harassment and assault. It operates 24/7 and offers counseling, crisis intervention, and referrals to local support services.

Shakti Shalini:

Shakti Shalini is an NGO based in Delhi that works on issues related to domestic violence and women's rights. They provide shelter, counseling, legal aid, and rehabilitation services to survivors of domestic abuse and sexual violence.

Men Against Violence and Abuse (MAVA): MAVA

MAVA is an organization based in Mumbai that engages men and boys in efforts to prevent gender-based violence, including sexual harassment and assault. They conduct workshops, campaigns, and advocacy initiatives to promote consent education and gender equality.
