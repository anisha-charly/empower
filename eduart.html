The Importance of Affirmative Consent:

Affirmative consent emphasizes the importance of enthusiastic and ongoing agreement in sexual encounters. It requires clear communication and mutual understanding between partners, ensuring that both parties actively consent to every step of the interaction. Affirmative consent shifts the focus from assuming consent to actively seeking it, fostering trust and respect in intimate relationships.
